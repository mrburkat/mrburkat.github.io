Valencia's Weather

Here in Valencia we have amazing weather. In the summer and srping we have the most perfect sun and weather for a nice tan beach and or pool, and other outside activities. While at night it cools off enough to be able to go out and relax.

Winter and fall has nice cool weather. You can go outside and enjoy the cool weather without having to sweat so much from the sun beaming down on you.
