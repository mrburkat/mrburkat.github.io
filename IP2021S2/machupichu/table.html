How Much it Costs to Travel to Machu Picchu

  • The prices of flights to Peru depend on where you're coming form. In any case, the destination is the Jorge Chávez airport in Lima. From there you must take another flight to the city of Cusco.

  • Tickets to Machu Picchu must be booked online and in advance due to limited availability. There is an official site that sells tickets.
Airlines Price (Lima-Cusco-Lima) Age Price (Admission to Machu Picchu)
Peruvian Airlines $93 USD Adult (Foreign) 152 S/
LATAM $90 USD Student (Foreign) 77 S/
Avianca $121 USD Child (Foreign) 77 S/
Star Peru $150 USD Adult (National) 64 S/
LC Peru $152 USD Child (National) 32 S/
S/ = Peruvian Sol, 1 Sol = 0.26 USD
