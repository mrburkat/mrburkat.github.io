Explore Everything Lisboa Has to Offer!

Lisboa has so many things to explore these are out top picks!

Castles

Castles are a major part of Lisboa's history and culture today. If you ever get the chance to take a tour around the beautiful castles we 100% recommend it.
  • Pena Palace
  • Guimaraes Castle
  • Tomar Castle
  • Castelo de Santa Clause Maria da Feira
  • Castelo de S. Jorge

Food

Anyone hungry? If so here are some must have foods to try before the end of your trip!
  • Patel de nata
  • Bifanas
  • Caldo Verde
  • Cataplana de mariscos
  • arroz de mariscos

Attractions and Experiences

Castles are one of the many attractions to see in Lisboa however, there are other great attractions to see while in the city.
  • Belém Tower
  • Pink Street
  • Padrão dos Descobrimentos
  • Praça do comércio
  • Street Art Stroll
