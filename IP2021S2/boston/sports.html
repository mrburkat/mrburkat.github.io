Boston Sports Teams

Boston Celtics

  • The Boston Celtics are my favorite sports team of all time and for many different reasons. Not only are they a team with so much history and background but currently they have a bunch of young stars that can get good and be powerhouses in the NBA.
  • They have 17 NBA Championship Wins and are tied with the Lakers for the most Championship wins in the NBA. They have 36 players currently in the Hall of Fame as well as 6 coaches.
  • They have retired 22 numbers and when you retire a number that means that no future player is allowed to use the number. That is such an honor for an NBA player and it really immortalizes you because you know no one else can use that number.

    • Boston Bruins

  • The Boston Bruins are the hockey team that play in Boston at TD Garden just like the Celtics.The Bruins have won the Stanley Cup 6 times with the most recent being in the 2010-2011 season.
  • They have won the O'Brien Cup 2 times which means they went to the Championship but didn't win. They have won the Prince of Wales Trophy which is Division Winner Trophy 18 times. And lastly they have won the Presidents' Trophy 3 times which is the most scored points in the regular season.
  • They have 57 players currently in the Hall of Fame and they have also had many All-Star players over the years.

    • New England Patriots

  • The New England Patriots are arguably one of the best football teams of all time. They currently play in Gilette Stadium which is 22 miles from downtown Boston located in Foxborough, Massachusetts.
  • They have won 6 Super Bowls, 11 Conference Championships, 22 Division Championships,and have had 28 Playoff Appearances.
  • The Patriots have retired 7 numbers and have 28 former players in the Hall of Fame. Also, the greatest football player of all time, Tom Brady, played for the Patriots. And finally, they have a huge rivalry with the New York Jets.
