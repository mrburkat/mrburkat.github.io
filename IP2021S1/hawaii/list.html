Activities in Maui

  • Road to Hana

    - A 64.4 mile long road feauturing secluded waterfalls, tropical rainforests, wild animals, and Hawaii's greatest natural wonders

  • Volcano Tours

    - An opportunity to visit Haleakala and Hawaii Volcanoes

  • Beaches of Maui

    - Visit the many beaches of Maui, there are many to choose from

  • Wailea Golf Club

    - Wailea's own golf club which includes their own restaurant and shop

  • Shops at Wailea

    - A shopping mall including over 70 shops, various restaurants, and art galleries

  • Wailer's Village (Lahaina)

    - A vibrant variety of 90 fine shops, restaurants, and a renowned whale museum

  • Pride of Maui Snorkel Tour

    - See various sea animals including sea turtles, baby sharks, a variety of fish, and more mysterious creatures of Maui

  • Whale Watching

    - A great opportunity to see real whales in the ocean on a ship

  • Go to a Luau

    - Enjoy a night of food, Maui traditions, and music by attending a Luau

  • Maui Surf Lessons

    - Offers private surf lessons, stand up paddleboarding, and canoe tours on Maui
