Attractions Galore!


Pavillion Park

This is Pavillion Park! It is a historic amusement park opened in 1948 located in Broadway at the Beach. Depicted is their well known ferris wheel - visible from the higway - the Broadway 360 Observation Wheel. At night, as shown, the ferris wheel lights up and displays a multitude of colors - truly eye candy! The park hosts a variety of other rides as well including:

  • The Beast
  • Wave Swinger
  • American Swing
  • Speed XXL

Great for families and thrill seekers!


Hollywood Wax Museum

This is the Ripley's Hollywood Wax Museum, where you can "visit" your favorite celebrities and take photos with them. All figures are exact replicas of the real celebrity, made of wax. They have people ranging from pop culture to history. People recreated include:

  • Snoop Dog
  • Rihanna
  • Katy Perry
  • Lady Gaga
  • Jason (Friday the 13th)
  • And many more!

It's the perfect way to get away from the summer heat and relax.


Brookgreen Gardens

Founded in 1931, this Historical National Landmark is a great way to get closer to nature and appreciate art. The gardens host a variety of gorgeous sculptures implemented into the flora and fauna of the gardens. It's an excellent and relaxing way to get some exercise or fresh air. It's an ideal place for families, art enthusiasts, and plant lovers alike!
