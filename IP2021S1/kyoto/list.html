Kyoto at a Glance...

Kyoto was the former capital of Japan for more than a thousand years. It is the center of traditonal Japanese culture. For a traveler, Kyoto is a place of endless fascination and delight.

Location:

  • West-central Honshu
  • Easily reached from Osaka International Airport
  • Or three hours by superexpress train fron Tokyo

Atmosphere and Life

Kyoto forms part of a large urban complex referred to as the Kinki region, which includes Osaka and Kobe as well. Kyoto preserves its historical charm and beauty in:

  1. Temples
  2. Shrines
  3. Parks
  4. Gardens
  5. Machiya, traditional wooden townhouses found throughout Japan

When to visit Kyoto:

  • March to May and September to November: weather is mildest
  • The blooming cherry bloosoms in spring and foliage of fall are big tourist draws, so be ready for higher rates and fewer vacancies.
  • SUmmer and winter: crowds wane. June's humidity and January's cold temperatures may be too uncomfortable.
