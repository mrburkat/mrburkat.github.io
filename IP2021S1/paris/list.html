Bucket List

  • Eiffel Tower
    • The most reconizable land mark of Paris, your first trip to Paris would not be complete without seeing it!
  • The Louvre
    • If you would like to learn about the history of Paris through art then The Louvre is for you! It is the worlds largest art museum and is home to the Mona Lisa.
  • Champ-Elysees
    • A walkable street that goes through Paris connecting Place de la Concorde and Arc de Triomphe. Along the way you can shop, take in the beautiful sight and stop for macaroons.
  • Arc De Triomphe
    • On one end of Champ-Elysees is Arc de Triomphe, where you can get one of the best views in Paris. You can see the Louvre, The Effiel tower and the rest of Paris just from one spot.
  • Ile De La Cite
    • A small island in the heart of Paris that is home to Sainte-Chapelle and Notre Dame
