Paris France

Paris, the French capital city and surely one of the most interesting city in the world. With its museums and monuments, fine boulevards, culture, restaurants and its unique atmosphere, you would never get tired of Paris. In the memorable words of Thomas Jefferson, "A walk about Paris will provide lessons in history, beauty, and in the point of life." And much more too. But Paris is just a starting point. France is much so much more than just Paris.
