A beautiful Spring day in Ennis, Montana

This picture was taken on the Main Street bridge over the Madison River in Ennis. There is a lone fisherman in the center that is fly fishing on the river, the most common passitme of residents of Ennis. In the background you can see the Madison mountian range with its snowcapped peaks that still haven't melted due to it being early spring.
