A gorgeous, cold winter day on the Madison in Ennis

In winter, Montana becomes a icy wonderland, with the average temperature in the town being 26 degrees, and even colder in the mountians. There is always a lot of snow on the ground, due to it being so far north. If you find it weird that the Madison River (Pictured) isnt frozen over, it is becasue it is always flowing, bringing hot water from geothermal vents north. Thats right, the Madison River flows West then North, not South.
