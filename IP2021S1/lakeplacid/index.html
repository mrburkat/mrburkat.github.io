Welcome to Lake Placid!

forever wild.

Lake Placid is located in the Adirondacks in New York. It is home to the Ironman, the 1932 Winter Olympics, and the 1980 Winter Olympics. Other events occur and it is a hidden gem. Some celebrities have come across this hidden gem like Bruce Springsteen, Shania Twain, and Lana Del Ray. It is a place to see and dwell in the beautiful nature.
