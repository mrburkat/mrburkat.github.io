The Ironman Table

The Ironman is held almost every year at Lake Placid. The 2.4 mile swim goes across Mirror Lake. Followed right after is the 112 mile bike ride that goes across the mountain roads around Lake Placid. Then finally, a 26.2 mile run to finish it off. Each of the highlighted boxes are the best times in each event and the overall event.
